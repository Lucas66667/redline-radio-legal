Redline Radio Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Redline Radio is a radio streaming app that helps you discover and listen to publicly available internet radio stations. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Redline Radio does not sell personal data, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, payment information, or advertising identifiers.

Information Stored on Your Device

Redline Radio stores app preferences and listening-related state locally on your device, such as favorites, recently played stations, song history, theme preference, language preference, last selected category, and sleep timer settings. This information is stored using iOS local storage such as UserDefaults and is used only to make the app work as expected.

Network Requests

To provide radio discovery and playback, the app may request station directory data from radio-browser API endpoints and may connect directly to third-party radio stream URLs selected in the app. These services may receive standard technical information from your device, such as IP address, user agent, request time, and the stream or endpoint requested. Redline Radio does not control the privacy practices of independent radio stations or third-party directory services.

Audio Playback

Redline Radio plays public internet radio streams. The app does not host, create, or modify the audio content provided by third-party stations.

Analytics and Advertising

Redline Radio does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

Redline Radio is not directed to children. Because the app provides access to public internet radio streams that are not pre-screened by us, users should use appropriate discretion when selecting stations.

Data Deletion

Because Redline Radio does not maintain a user account or server-side user profile, there is no account data to request for deletion. You can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channel listed for Redline Radio on the App Store.
