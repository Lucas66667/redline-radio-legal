Redline Radio Support

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Redline Radio helps you discover and listen to publicly available internet radio stations. This page explains how to get help and what to check if playback or station discovery is not working as expected.

Redline Radio does not host radio streams. Station availability, metadata, logos, and audio content are provided by independent third-party stations and public directory services.

Common Issues

  • Check that your device has a stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection.
  • If a station does not play, try another station. Some streams may be offline or region-limited.
  • If playback stops in the background, reopen the app and start the station again.
  • If app data seems stale, quit and relaunch Redline Radio.

Feature Notes

Redline Radio supports favorites, recent stations, playback history, language selection, sleep timer, and background audio playback. The app currently does not offer subscriptions, in-app purchases, or paid premium features.

Contact

For support, please use the contact or support channel listed for Redline Radio on the App Store. When reporting a problem, include your device model, iOS version, the station name, and a short description of what happened.
